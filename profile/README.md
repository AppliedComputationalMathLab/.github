# Applied Computational Math Lab (ACML)

> Open research organization dedicated to Computational Mathematics, Scientific Computing, Numerical Methods, and Computational Engineering.

## About

The Applied Computational Math Lab (ACML) is an academic initiative focused on the development of research, software, educational resources, and computational tools related to applied mathematics and scientific computing.

Our mission is to investigate how computational techniques can be used to model, simulate, analyze, and solve real-world problems arising in science, engineering, and technology.

The organization serves as a collaborative space for students, researchers, and contributors interested in computational approaches to mathematics and engineering.
